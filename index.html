Compute and Create Club

Welcome to the Compute and Create Club! We are a dynamic community of passionate tech enthusiasts and aspiring innovators within the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science).

I'm Interested!

Who We Are

Our mission is to explore the realms of mathematics, programming, and technology to foster creativity, innovation, and collaboration. With a focus on our academic subjects, we offer a diverse range of activities, including workshops, coding challenges, tech talks, and hands-on labs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find CACC?

Find CACC meetings are announced via our WhatsApp Group!. Click Me!

When are meetings?

Meetings take place every Saturday at 10:30 AM.

Who can participate?

All current students of RVR & JCCE can join CACC! If you're not a student from RVR & JCCE, feel free to drop by and check out what we're doing.

Do I need to know how to code to be in CACC?

Not at all! Our workshops are super beginner-friendly and we encourage more people to learn how to code. No programming experience required.
