About Us

What We Do


We are a dynamic community of passionate tech enthusiasts and aspiring innovators within the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science). Our mission is to explore the realms of mathematics, programming, and technology to foster creativity, innovation, and collaboration. With a focus on our academic subjects, we offer a diverse range of activities, including workshops, coding challenges, tech talks, and hands-on labs..

Our club provides members with a platform to enhance their skills, tackle real-world problems, and network with like-minded individuals. Whether you're a coding wizard, a data visualization enthusiast, or simply curious about the world of technology, our club is a place where you can learn, create, and inspire. Join us as we embark on a journey to push the boundaries of what's possible in the ever-evolving world of computing and data science.
